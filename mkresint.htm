Research Interests

      • 7. Normal Forms for Discrete Dynamical Systems
      • 8. Mathematical Modelling That Involves any of the Equations from Above.
      • 9. CAS (Computer Algebra System) Implementation of Some Recent Research Results from 1.-5.
      • 10. WEB Implementation of Some  Research Results from 1.-5.
